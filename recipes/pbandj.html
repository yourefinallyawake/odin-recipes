Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich


Do you sometimes feel like you don't deserve nice things in life? I know I certainly do!

One of those days you don't feel like treating yourself to some wholesome healthy balanced meal.

Then why not just slab some textured goo between two pieces of bread and call it a day?

    Ingredients:
  • Bread: white, factory-made, pre-sliced
  • Peanut Butter, plain for good experience, crunchy if you're a psychopath
  • Jam, jelly, marmelade, honey, chocolate spread, basically anything you can get your mitts on
    Steps
  1. Make sure you get all those ingredients at home, otherwise go out and get it and I don't care how you do it, just do it, for God's sake!
  2. You'll need a knife or a spoon to spread the goo onto the bread. Don't look so surprised.
  3. You may toast the bread or just leave it the way it was born.
  4. Slab some peanut butter on one slice, jam up the other one.
  5. Put the slices together. Good job.
